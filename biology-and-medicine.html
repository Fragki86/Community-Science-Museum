Biology and Medicine

Human Biology

Learn how hormones affect your body, how senses influence perception, how our memories function, and how the brain and other organs work together. See giant blood cells, hear a baby in the womb, and test your memory skills in this interactive gallery.

Different exhibits will show you a real sample of DNA and a giant cell model to see what the different parts of it are doing.

After your journey, have fun by testing your memory and see if you can survive the "Staying Alive" games.

Medical Science

Learn how the medical science has affected the life expectancy during the years and how the understanding of this have helped people to not being in so much pain any more.

In the historical timeline of medicine you will see how radical were the changes from era to era and press the highlighted sections of the interactive board for a deeper explanation and description.

Discover how the penicillin was founded and how this breakthrough, among others, made a huge impact in every aspect of medicine and human life.

The first heart transplant section is one of the top visited section of our museum and really interesting and impressive topic. See how it happened and how this technic has progressed through the years and has achieved to save thousands of people every year.
