Renewable Energy

Sources

Observe how different sources around us can produce energy and how they are being used around the world, depending on the needs and the natural sources of each country.

Around this exhibition we have created our own mini energy plants, including wind, solar and hydro plants.

Experience how a house can be totally independent and generate it's own electricity by having the proper solar panel blocks. Beside that, you can learn how some countries have created solar farms to generate power for thousands of homes, or some others that they use hydroelectric power.

Activities

We have created some video games that will help you understand better how the different sources of renewable energy are working.

'Power Up' will put you in a state to gather the wind by moving your wind turbine up and down, and moving your solar panels towards the sun to gather sunlight. By achieving these, your goal is to power up your digital town.

Another game is called 'Offset' and is made to show you how the energy production of our days, affects extremely the environment and it will show you visually, the differences between renewable and non energy.
