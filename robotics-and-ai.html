Robotics and AI

Robotics

Experience and discover one of the top future sciences. Rob the Robot will guide you around this section of the museum and will show you the evolution of robotics during the years. You can even interact with it and ask him questions about the section and his functions. You will be surprised of what this very kind robot of ours is capable of knowing and answering.

Rob will show you how pieces of 'scrap-metal' are created to operative programmed objects and how the scientists are making them functionable.

Robots, of course, are not only to have fun with. Discover the impact they have on industry and medicine, as well as the dangers that are hiding underneath this science.

Artificial Intelligence

Without the artificial intelligence, robots would almost not exist. AI is a very interesting and funny science. Discover and interact with a variety of home appliances and software applications that will be very useful in the upcoming future.

Participating in creative programming activities in which you will be shown how to build games, program robots, and train your own AI models.

In this section of the museum you are going to cultivate curiosity and grit, and we have made this exhibition very clear for the children to understand the fundamental of AI.

This is happening by having an interactive dialog with our AI build-in system, that the children can ask and get answers and examples. They will experience as well how the machine are learning new things and why and how people came with the ideas of creating AI.
